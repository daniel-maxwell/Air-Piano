Air Piano

Air Piano uses movement detection techniques to allow you to play the piano with nothing but your camera!


To get started, click 'OK' on the pop-up to allow to access your webcam.


Then, set the movement detection threshold to suit your camera and environment.

Note: Setting the threshold extremely low will trigger a lot of animations and may affect performance.


Finally, select the musical scale you'd like to play in. More on scales here.


To find out more about this project, check out the Github repo.
